Personalized Health Monitoring System for Managing Well-Being in Rural Areas

  • 发表年份：2017
  • 发表期刊：Journal of Medical Systems
  • 影响因子：2.456

Background

  • Rural India lacks easy access to health practitioners and medical centers, depending instead on community health workers
  • The introduction of wearable health devices has made it easier to monitor health conditions
  • However, existing initiatives have not succeeded in providing adequate health monitoring to rural and low-literate patients

Aim

This patient-centric integrated healthcare system is designed to:

  • manage the overall health of villagers with real-time health monitoring of patients
  • offer guidance on preventive care
  • increase health awareness and self-monitoring at an affordable price

Method

  • Providing low-cost medical devices connected to a low-cost health platform
  • Personalized guidance based on patient physiological parameters in local languages
  • Alerts to medical practitioners in case of emergencies

Result

This personalized health monitoring system addresses the health-related needs in remote and rural areas by:

  • empowering health workers in monitoring of basic health conditions for rural patients in order to prevent escalations
  • personalized feedback regarding nutrition, exercise, diet, preventive Ayurveda care and yoga postures based on vital parameters
  • reporting of patient data to the patient’s health center with emergency alerts to doctor and patient
